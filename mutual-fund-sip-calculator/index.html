About SIP Calculator

What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing in mutual funds where you invest a fixed amount at regular intervals. It helps in rupee-cost averaging and disciplined investing.

Benefits of SIP

  • • Rupee cost averaging
  • • Power of compounding
  • • Disciplined investing
  • • Lower risk through diversification

How is SIP calculated?

Our calculator uses the compound interest formula:
FV = P × [((1 + r)^n - 1) / r] × (1 + r)

Where:
FV = Future Value
P = Monthly investment amount
r = Monthly interest rate
n = Number of months
